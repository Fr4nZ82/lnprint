Step by step guide

Optional: open a channel with our node

Obviosly, for payments, you can use your already existing channels; open a new channel with our node, owever, increase your and us LN routing capability. If You deposit satoshis and let them in your account you can use the channel for routing purpose. You can retrive your unspent satoshis whenever you want without any additional costs. Incrase the routing capability of the network is good for you, for us, and for all LN users!

Generate a new key

Note that this is a real bitcoin private key, we do not save it in the database but we save the correspondings public key and btc address. Copy the private key and save it in a safe place. This key is not used for payments in the application; we use it as a login key and, if you don't want to reveal your public btc address we can use it (or any other address you want) in case of refounds, if is not possible to use LN.

Select a product and upload your pdf

Each product has different specifications and prices. Note that also the characteristics of the pdf you will upload will change according to the type of product.
Select the various options, write special requests in the appropriate field. The email is not mandatory, but you can enter one to receive notifications and drafts.
Take note of the unitary cost of your printings, you may need to know the total cost of your work to open a channel with the appropriate amount of satoshis.
We need a small payment of 1$ to accept your pdf; This is because to print the draft and send you the picture there is a little work to do. Also without this payment anyone could upload pdf, make us do this work and then disappear.

Wait the draft

At this point we send the work to the typography and in few hours the draft will be ready. If you have specified the email we will send you a notification, otherwise you can see your draft in your dashboard.

Accept or decline

If you accept the draft specifies how many copies you want and pay the work, otherwise you can tell us what to modify and / or send another pdf and request another draft (the second draft is always free);

Wait the production

You can see the status of your print in your dashboard. If you have specified the email, a notification will be sent to you at the end of the job.

Shipping

Decide on the shipping method, fill in the forum with your address and pay (this can also be done during production). As soon the work and the payment is done, we send you the package.

Print more!!

At any time you can print other copies of a work already done, there are no limits to the number of copies. For example, if you printed 1000 flyers and you realize you need another 100: no problem, reopen the work and order another 100 copies by paying the invoice.
